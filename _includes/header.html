State of the Map 2021

There are several different ways to attend SotM 2021:

  • Got a ticket? Join the interactive virtual conference platform over at venueless. You received a personal invite link with your ticket.
  • The main track will be live streamed publicly.
  • Follow @SotM on Twitter and use the hashtag #sotm2021 when tweeting.

Please respect the conference's code of conduct. If you encounter any problems, don't hesitate to contact the team via Telegram or email to receive the personal contact details of our code of conduct team.
